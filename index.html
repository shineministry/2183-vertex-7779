Secure Digital Documents

The Online Vault is a fully encrypted document management platform — store, view, share, and organize your most sensitive files with military-grade protection. No data leaves your control.

Everything You Need in One Secure Place

AES-256 Encryption
Every document is encrypted with AES-256-GCM before storage. Your password is hashed with PBKDF2 — not even we can read your files.
Multi-Category Organization
Organize documents into categories like Identity, Finance, Medical, Education, Legal, and more. Find anything instantly with live search.
Family Member Profiles
Store documents for the whole family — each member gets their own profile, file set, and password manager entries, all in one vault.
Built-in PDF Viewer
View PDFs directly in your browser with a secure, encrypted streaming viewer. No downloads, no third-party apps required.
Secure Share Links
Generate time-limited, password-protected share links for any document. AES-256 encrypted end-to-end with auto-expiry.
Offline Access
Documents are cached locally for offline viewing. Access your vault even without internet — perfect for travel or low-connectivity areas.
Password Manager
Built-in encrypted password manager per family member. Store, generate, and autofill credentials securely within the vault.
Document Compare & More
Compare two documents side by side, pin favourites, track access logs, and personalize with themes, animations, and 40+ advanced settings.

Military-Grade Protection

Your data is encrypted before it leaves your device. We use AES-256-GCM, PBKDF2 key derivation, and SHA-256 hashing — the same standards used by banks and governments worldwide.
